Generate the ASSESSMENT section of a SOAP note based on the provided subjective and objective information. Include:

1. Primary diagnosis or clinical impression with supporting evidence
2. Differential diagnoses if applicable
3. Analysis of findings explaining:
   - Correlation between subjective complaints and objective findings
   - Functional limitations and their impact
   - Disease/condition progression or improvement since previous visits
   - Current level of function or disability
4. Prognosis with rationale
5. Identification of barriers to recovery or contributing factors
6. Clinical reasoning supporting your assessment

Provide a clear, concise analysis that demonstrates critical thinking and connects the patient's presentation to clinical decision-making.
