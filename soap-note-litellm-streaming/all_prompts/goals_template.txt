Generate the GOALS section of a SOAP note based on the provided assessment and plan. Include:

1. Short-term goals (1-2 weeks):
   - Specific, measurable objectives
   - Functional improvements expected
   - Pain reduction targets if applicable
2. Long-term goals (treatment duration):
   - Return to specific activities/functions
   - Self-management capabilities
   - Independence levels
3. Measurable outcomes with timeframes:
   - Objective markers of progress
   - Functional assessment targets
   - Quality of life improvements
4. Patient's personal goals incorporated into the plan
5. Rehabilitation and recovery milestones

Ensure all goals are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and directly connect to the patient's condition and needs.
