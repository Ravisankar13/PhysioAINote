Generate the OBJECTIVE section of a SOAP note based on the provided patient information. Include:

1. Vital signs (if available)
2. Physical examination findings organized by body systems:
   - General appearance
   - Specific findings related to the chief complaint
   - Relevant negative findings
   - Functional assessments where applicable
3. Observable measurements including:
   - Range of motion values
   - Strength measurements
   - Pain scales with provocation testing
   - Functional outcome measures
4. Results of any diagnostic tests performed
5. Objective observations of patient behavior, movement patterns, or limitations

Focus exclusively on measurable, observable data without interpretation. Present information in a structured format with clear, precise clinical language.
