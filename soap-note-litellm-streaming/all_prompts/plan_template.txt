Generate the PLAN section of a SOAP note based on the provided assessment. Include:

1. Detailed treatment plan with:
   - Specific interventions and their frequency/duration
   - Medications with dosage, frequency, and duration if applicable
   - Home program recommendations
   - Referrals to specialists or services
2. Patient education provided:
   - Condition explanation
   - Self-management strategies
   - Activity modifications
3. Follow-up schedule and criteria:
   - Timeframe for next appointment
   - Parameters for monitoring progress
   - Criteria for discharge or treatment modification
4. Coordination of care with other providers
5. Orders for additional diagnostic tests if needed

Present this information in a structured, actionable format that clearly outlines next steps for both provider and patient.
