Generate the SUBJECTIVE section of a SOAP note based on the provided patient information. Include:

1. Chief complaint (CC) in patient's own words
2. History of present illness (HPI) with relevant details:
   - Onset, duration, and progression
   - Location and radiation
   - Quality and severity
   - Aggravating and alleviating factors
   - Associated symptoms
3. Review of systems (ROS) relevant to the presenting complaint
4. Past medical/surgical history if relevant to current complaint
5. Current medications and allergies if pertinent
6. Social history elements affecting current condition

Format this as a concise narrative paragraph that captures the patient's perspective and experience. Focus only on subjective information reported by the patient.
