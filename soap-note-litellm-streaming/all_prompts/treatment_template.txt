Generate the TREATMENT section of a SOAP note based on the provided assessment and patient information. Include:

1. Treatment plan overview:
   - Short-term and long-term goals
   - Estimated duration of treatment
   - Frequency of sessions

2. Specific interventions:
   - Manual therapy techniques (if applicable)
   - Therapeutic exercises
   - Modalities (e.g., heat, ice, electrical stimulation)
   - Patient education topics

3. Home exercise program:
   - Detailed instructions for exercises
   - Frequency and duration recommendations

4. Lifestyle modifications or activity restrictions

5. Referrals or consultations (if necessary):
   - Other healthcare professionals
   - Diagnostic tests

6. Medications (if within scope of practice):
   - Recommendations for over-the-counter medications
   - Referral to physician for prescription medications if needed

7. Follow-up plan:
   - Next appointment scheduling
   - Criteria for re-evaluation

8. Patient education:
   - Explanation of treatment rationale
   - Self-management strategies
   - Warning signs to watch for

9. Precautions and contraindications specific to this patient

10. Expected outcomes and timeline for improvement

11. Plan for measuring and documenting progress

Ensure the treatment plan is evidence-based, patient-centered, and directly addresses the issues identified in the assessment. Provide clear reasoning for each component of the treatment plan, demonstrating how it relates to the patient's condition and goals.
